Lemon Meringue Pie Recipe

Prep Time: 30 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made lemon pie base from Granny's Pie
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tablespoons white sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven: Set your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare the Lemon Filling: In a saucepan, whisk together sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt. Stir in water, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Bring to a boil, then whisk in the butter.
  3. Add the Egg Yolks: Slowly mix some of the hot sugar mixture into the beaten egg yolks, then return everything to the saucepan. Cook until thickened, then pour into the pie base.
  4. Make the Meringue: Beat egg whites until foamy. Gradually add sugar, beating until stiff peaks form.
  5. Top the Pie: Spread the meringue over the lemon filling, sealing it to the edges of the crust.
  6. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the meringue is golden brown.
  7. Cool & Serve: Let the pie cool before serving. Enjoy!
